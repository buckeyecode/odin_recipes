Blueberry BBQ Sauce

Ingredients

  • 4 C. Blueberries
  • 1/2 C. Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 1/4 C. Brown Sugar
  • 1 T. Grated Ginger
  • 1 T. Molasses
  • 1 t. Salt
  • 1 t. Chili Powder
  • 1/4 t. Cinnamon
  • 1/4 t. Allspice

Directions

  1. Combin all ingredients in a sauce pan on the stove-top.
  2. Heat on medium until bubbling & the blueberries have broken down. If more liquid is needed, add orange juice or water.
  3. Strain or processess the sauce while warm to get a smoother consistancy. Leave "as is" to cool for a more rustic texture.
