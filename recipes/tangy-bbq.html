Tangy BBQ Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 T. Butter
  • 1 Grated Onion
  • 2 C. Ketchup
  • 1/2 C. Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 1/4 C. Water
  • 1/4 C. Apple Juice
  • 1/4 C. Worchestershire Sauce
  • 2 T. Brown Sugar
  • 2 T. Molasses
  • 2 T. Honey
  • 2 t. Dry Mustard
  • 1 t. Chili Powder
  • 1 t. Garlic Powder
  • 1 t. Cayenne Powder

Directions

  1. Combin all ingredients in a sauce pan. On stove-top, heat on medium until bubbling.
  2. Let cool.
