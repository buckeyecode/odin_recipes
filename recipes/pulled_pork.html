Pulled Pork

Equipment

  • Electric Smoker
  • Apple Wood
  • Probe Thermometer

Ingredients

  • 8 lbs. Pork Butt
  • 1/4 C. Yellow Mustard
  • Rub
  • Apple Juice

Directions

  1. Bring the pork to room temperature, then pat dry with paper towels. Rub the mustard covering the pork. Liberally apply the rub to the pork & rub! Cover in foil & refrigerate over night.
  2. Set electric smoker to 275 degrees F. Fill the wood chip tray with the apple wood & the drip pan with the apple juice. Preheat with vent completely open.
  3. Remove pork from refrigerate & uncover the foil. Place pork on smoker rack with the fatty side on top. Place in smoker & smoke for 4 hours, uninterrupted.
  4. After 4 hours, remove pork from smoker. Wrap in foil & place thermometer probe into the center of the pork. Return pork to smoker. Adjust the vent to 1/2 open & smoke until the internal temperature reads 200 degrees F.
  5. Once the internal temperature holds at 190, remove the pork from the smoker. Rest 30 minutes in a pan, covered by a dish towel.
  6. Remove the pork to the serving dish. Remove from the foil & shread using forks or your hands.

Enjoy with one of these sauces!!!

  • Blueberry
  • Gold
  • Tangy
